1. Product Vision

What Sambla Really Is

Sambla is an AI employee for your business. Not a chatbot. Not a widget. An employee that knows everything about your business, talks to your customers 24/7 on any channel, and gets smarter every day.

Core User Journeys (Prioritized)

  1. Sign up → first working bot in 10 minutes. The entire onboarding must fit in a single focused session.
  2. Daily check: "How is my AI doing?" Dashboard answers this in 5 seconds with one glance.
  3. Weekly improvement: "What should I add?" Knowledge gaps + auto-suggestions make this effortless.
  4. Customer experience: "This chatbot actually helps." Accurate answers, no hallucination, natural conversation.

What Makes Sambla Different (Positioning)

Learns Your Business

Not generic AI. Answers from YOUR documents, products, and policies. Never invents.

Voice + Chat, One Brain

Customers call or write — same quality. One system, all channels.

Gets Smarter Alone

Identifies what it doesn't know, suggests content to add, improves automatically.

3. Onboarding Flow (Complete Redesign)

Goal: From signup to first working bot in under 10 minutes. No technical knowledge required.

Flow Steps

1

Sign Up (30 seconds)

Name, email, password. No company details yet. No credit card. Instant access.

After signup: Redirect directly to setup wizard, NOT to empty dashboard.

2

Choose Your Business Type (15 seconds)

Three large, clear cards with illustrations:

  • E-commerce — "Vinzi produse online" (You sell products online)
  • Servicii — "Oferi servicii sau consultanță" (You offer services or consulting)
  • Ambele — "Vinzi produse ȘI oferi servicii" (Both products and services)

Effect: Pre-fills system prompt template, KB structure, recommended settings, and ConversationPolicy defaults.

3

Describe Your Business (60 seconds)

Single textarea: "Descrie afacerea ta în 2-3 propoziții"

Examples shown as placeholder: "Suntem un magazin online de materiale de construcții cu livrare în toată țara. Avem peste 5000 de produse și oferim consultanță gratuită."

AI generates: System prompt, greeting message, bot name suggestion. User reviews and approves.

4

Add Your Website (90 seconds)

Enter domain → auto-verification → one-click site scan option.

"Vrei să scanăm site-ul pentru informații? Bot-ul va învăța automat din conținutul tău."

Optional: Connect WooCommerce (if e-commerce was selected)

5

Build Knowledge Base (3-5 minutes)

Guided checklist based on business type. Each item is a one-click action:

  • 📄 Upload policy documents (drag & drop)
  • ❓ Generate FAQ from website (AI agent, one click)
  • 📞 Add contact info (simple form: phone, email, address, hours)
  • 🛒 Sync products (if WooCommerce connected)

Show live completeness bar: "Bot-ul tău știe 65% din ce trebuie"

6

Test Your Bot (60 seconds)

Embedded chat panel on the RIGHT side. Pre-filled suggested questions on the LEFT.

"Întreabă bot-ul tău: Cât costă livrarea? / Ce program aveți? / Vreau să comand"

If bot can't answer → highlight which KB content is missing → one-click add.

This is the magic moment. User sees their bot actually knowing their business.

7

Go Live (30 seconds)

Show embed code. Copy button. "Adaugă acest cod pe site-ul tău."

Or: "Instalează plugin-ul WordPress" with one-click download.

Confetti animation. "Bot-ul tău e live! 🎉"

6. Super Admin Panel V2

Overview Dashboard

  • Global metrics: Total tenants, active bots, conversations today, revenue this month
  • Health map: All bots by health score (heatmap/grid). Red = needs attention, green = healthy.
  • Alerts: Bots with high fallback rates, high frustration, error spikes.
  • Recent activity: Last 10 tenant actions (signup, bot created, KB updated).

Tenant Detail

  • Tenant info + plan + usage
  • All bots with health scores
  • Revenue and costs breakdown
  • AI quality score (aggregate of all bots)
  • Plan override controls

11. QA Review

What is NOT yet WOW

  • No video content. Every premium SaaS has a product video. Sambla has none. A 60-second demo video on the homepage would be the single highest-impact addition.
  • No real testimonials with photos. Current testimonials are clearly fabricated names. Real customers with photos or nothing.
  • Blog is dead. Remove it from navigation until there's real content. A link to "/blog" that shows fake articles damages credibility.
  • Pricing page likely empty. Plans aren't seeded in production. This needs real pricing before any sales push.
  • No onboarding wizard exists yet. This is the #1 missing product piece. Users land on an empty dashboard and don't know what to do.
  • ConversationPolicy has no settings UI. The model exists, it's wired into PromptBuilder, but there's no page where admins can configure tone/verbosity/CTA.
  • No dark mode. Chat widget supports it, dashboard doesn't. Not critical but noticeable.
  • No streaming in chat. Users wait 2-5 seconds for full response. Streaming would feel instant.
  • Setup wizard doesn't exist. The most critical UX gap for conversion.
  • No mobile app or PWA. Dashboard on mobile is responsive but not optimized.

What IS Already WOW

  • 10-layer adaptive prompt system. Tone adapts to frustration, query type, conversation stage. Genuinely sophisticated.
  • Self-improving loop. AdaptiveLearning + KnowledgeGaps + AutoKB = platform gets smarter alone.
  • Voice + Chat parity. Same orchestrator, same quality. Unique differentiator.
  • Proactive hints on product pages. Context-aware, non-annoying, helpful.
  • Product card gating. Dual-signal check prevents irrelevant cards. Zero misleading behavior.
  • Health score + knowledge gaps in dashboard. Visible, actionable intelligence.
  • E-commerce depth. Product search, order lookup, cart attribution, purchase funnel. Way beyond typical chatbots.
  • Lead pipeline with CRM. Full funnel from conversation → lead → won/lost.

Summary

The backend intelligence is world-class. The gap is in product experience: onboarding wizard, KB builder UX, ConversationPolicy settings UI, and a convincing presentation website. Close these gaps and Sambla competes globally.
